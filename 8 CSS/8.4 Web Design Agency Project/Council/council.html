Community

The UMIT Student Council serves as the heartbeat of the college community, fostering unity and leadership among students. Committed to integrating academics and co-curricular activities, they represent and support their fellow students, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience. Through various cultural initiatives, the council forms a vibrant community that encourages personal growth and teamwork, strengthening the bonds that tie the student body together.

Events

The UMIT Student Council is the driving force behind a myriad of captivating events. Their annual cultural festival, 'Arcane Illusions,' transcends college boundaries, drawing participation from institutions across Mumbai. By organizing these events, the council not only creates memorable experiences but also instills valuable skills in their members. Their dedication to enhancing campus life and the cultural atmosphere ensures a rich tapestry of activities that enrich the student experience.
